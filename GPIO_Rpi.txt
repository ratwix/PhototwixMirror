# Connexion des GPIO
# Ecran
* Pin 4 (+5V) --> +5v ecran
* Pin 6 (GND) --> GND ecran

# Power Button
* Pin 5 (GPIO 3)  --> Power Button
* Pin 9 (GND)     --> Power Button

# Action Button
* Pin 3 (GIPO 2)  --> Action Button
* Pin 14 (GND)    --> Action Button

# Camera Flash
* Pin 2 (+5V) --> +5v flash
* Pin 6 (GND) --> GND Flash
* Pin 7 (GPIO 4) --> GPIO Flash
